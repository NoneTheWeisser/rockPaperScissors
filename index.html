How to play the game

  • Go to Chrome's console
    • To quickly open the Chrome Developer Console, use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + Shift + J (Windows/Linux) or Cmd + Opt + J (Mac)
    • OR- Right click and click on inspect.
  • Refresh your page
  • A prompt should appear
  • Enter in your choice!
